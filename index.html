About Me

I am currently in my third year of Software Engineering at the University of New Brunswick, Fredericton. Alongside my degree, I am in the Co-op program seeking interesting work opportunities, and also pursuing a diploma in Technology Management & Entrepreneurship.

In my free time, I like to attend video game tournaments for the game "Super Smash Brothers Melee". Besides being a die-hard competitor, I also help out with organizing tournaments. This provides me with a great way to grow my management and communication skills.

I am constantly looking to improve my day-to-day practices to be my best self I can be. I enjoy reading and listening to articles from blogs such as College Info Geek, with the goal of helping people become better students.
